Case File 001

The Secret Message

Location: 11-B

A strange message appeared on a student's desk.

The message said: "Meet me at 10."

No one knows who wrote it. The classroom was empty when teachers arrived.

Evidence

  • Mysterious note

    A note saying: “Meet me at 10.” was found on a classroom desk.

  • Broken clock

    The classroom clock stopped at 10:05 PM. Investigators believed it might be related to the incident.

  • Unknown handwriting

    The handwriting on the note looked similar to someone’s class notes.

Case File 002

The Missing Notebook

Location: School Libary

A student reported that an important notebook disappeared from the school library.

The strange thing is, the notebook was still there 10 minutes before it suddenly disappeared.

The CCTV stopped working at the same time.

Someone may be hiding a secret.

Evidence

  • Torn Paper Found

    A torn piece of paper was found near the classroom door.

  • A black pen on the floor

    A black pen was found on the classroom floor.

  • CCTV suddenly off

    The CCTV camera stopped working 10 minutes before the incident.

Case File 003

The Lost USB

Location: computer Lab

A USB containing important files disappeared from the computer lab.

Right before it went missing, the electricity suddenly turned off.

When the lights came back, the USB was gone.

Evidence

  • Computer still on

    One computer remained on after everyone left.

  • Chair moved

    A chair near the computer desk was found out of place.

  • Unknown login

    A strange login appeared in the computer system.
